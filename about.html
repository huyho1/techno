About Us

What is Technotonin?


Technotonin is a company that solves problems by bringing together a team of inventors to come up with solutions. The inventors work on prototypes for three months, and if necessary, more time is given to certain projects. Once a final prototype is made, Technotonin patents the invention and either licenses or sells it to another company. Technotonin also helps the partner company bring the product to market. In essence, Technotonin acts as a catalyst for innovation, identifying problems, fostering creativity, and bringing solutions to market.

Origin


On May 2, 2022, Technotonin Industries became an official LLC. It was founded in Franklin, MA by Antonio Marzarotti and Arav Tyagi and ever since then, it's grown significantly. It was first founded in hopes of creating innovative technology that could improve human life and as a tool for unestablished inventors to come up with new ideas for new inventions. Now, the most recent invention known as PAWE has gardnered major attention as it is revolutionary to the wheelchair attachment industry. As of now, the company is looking towards future opportunities with new inventions.



Unique Characteristics


  • Technotonin is a one-of-a-kind organization that specializes in finding creative and realistic solutions to real-world problems.
  • The solutions provided by Technotonin are not only creative but also affordable, efficient to produce, and of high quality. This ensures that the solutions are accessible to everyone who needs them.
  • The company's rapid prototyping process is designed to tackle spontaneous issues and find effective solutions quickly.
  • Technotonin provides inventors and idea-generators with access to resources and expertise, including prototyping, patenting, and licensing. This empowers individuals to bring their best ideas to life and profit from their contributions.
  • Technotonin's partnerships across multiple industries offer diversity and stability, making the company well-rounded and equipped to tackle a wide range of problems.
  • The company democratizes the product development process by making it accessible to everyone with a good idea, regardless of their background or resources.
  • Technotonin is dedicated to bringing new solutions to the world and improving people's lives, making the world a better place for everyone.
